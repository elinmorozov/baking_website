Teramesu


Ingredients:


  • Silken Tofu
  • 150g of Coconut Cream (from the top of the can)
  • 2 tbsp of Sugar
  • Lady Fingers
  • 1/2 Cup of Cocoa Drink or Coffee
  • Amaretto (alcohol)
  • Creme Freche
  • Double Creme
  • To Decorate: Sprinkle White and Dark Chocolate

Method:


  1. 2 Cups of Flour
  2. 1/2 Cups of Sugar
  3. 1 tsp of Baking Soda
  4. 1 tsp of Baking Powder
  5. 1 Cup of Soya Yogurt
  6. 1 tsp of Vanilla Extract
  7. 4 tbsp of Melted Margarin
  8. 3 tbsp Chocolate Powder
  9. Chocolate Chips (as many as you desire)
