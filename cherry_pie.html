Cherry Pie


Ingredients:


  • 2 Cups of All-Purpose Flour
  • A Dash of Salt
  • 1/2 Cup of Vegetable Oil (nutral taste)
  • 5 tbsp of Cold Icy Water
  • 5 Cups of Frozen Cherries (seed taken out)
  • Around 1/2 Cup of Cane Sugar
  • 4 tbsp of Corn Starch
  • 3 tbsp Chocolate Powder
  • 1 tsp of Vanilla Extract

Method:


  1. 2 Cups of Flour
  2. 1/2 Cups of Sugar
  3. 1 tsp of Baking Soda
  4. 1 tsp of Baking Powder
  5. 1 Cup of Soya Yogurt
  6. 1 tsp of Vanilla Extract
  7. 4 tbsp of Melted Margarin
  8. 3 tbsp Chocolate Powder
  9. Chocolate Chips (as many as you desire)
