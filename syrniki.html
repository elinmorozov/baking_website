Syrniki


Ingredients:


  • 275g of Quark
  • 20g of Flour
  • 1 tbsp of Sugar
  • 25g of Semolina
  • Egg Substitute
  • 1 tsp of Vanilla Extract

Method:


  1. Rub the quark through a large metal sieve.
  2. Mix together the egg substitute, semolina, flour, vanilla and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Add the quark and mix thoroughly into the mixture. Let the mixture to rest for 2 minutes.
  4. Lay out the mixture in some muffin tins.
  5. Put them into the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  6. Once they are finished baking, sprinkle them with some icing sugar and lay them out onto a plate.
