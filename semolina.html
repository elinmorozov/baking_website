Semolina Cake


Ingredients:


  • 1 Cup of Milk
  • 3 tbsp of Plain Yogurt
  • 1 Cup of Semolina
  • 1 Cup of Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup of Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Cup of Plain Flour
  • 2 tsp of Baking Powder
  • A Pinch of Salt
  • 1 tsp of Vanilla Extract
  • Optional: Raisins or Cranberries
  • Some Butter for the Tray
  • Chocolate Sauce: Chocolate, 5 tbsp of Milk, 1 tbsp of Powdered Sugar

Method:


  1. 2 Cups of Flour
  2. 1/2 Cups of Sugar
  3. 1 tsp of Baking Soda
  4. 1 tsp of Baking Powder
  5. 1 Cup of Soya Yogurt
  6. 1 tsp of Vanilla Extract
  7. 4 tbsp of Melted Margarin
  8. 3 tbsp Chocolate Powder
  9. Chocolate Chips (as many as you desire)
