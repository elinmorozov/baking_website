Austrain Dumplings


Ingredients:


  • 2 Egg Yolks or 1 and 1/2 tbsp Flaxseed
  • 70g of Butter or Margarine (room temperature)
  • 1 tbsp of Vanilla Extract
  • 2 tbsp of Sugar
  • 75g of All-Purpose Flour (extra for unsticking hands)
  • 20g of Semolina
  • 250g of Quark
  • A Pinch of Salt (for boiling water)
  • Filling Options: Apricots, Nougat, Chocolate, Chocolate Lindt Balls, Cherries
  • Coating: 7 tbsp of Breadcrumbs, 1 tbsp of Brown Sugar, Cinnamon
